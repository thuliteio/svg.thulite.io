FAQs

What does Thulite SVGs do?Thulite SVGs makes it easy to use, optimize, and integrate SVGs in your project.
Why use SVGs instead of images?SVGs are lightweight, scalable, and crisp at any size—ideal for icons and UI elements.
Do I need to optimize SVGs manually?No. SVGs are automatically optimized for efficient delivery.
Can I style SVGs with CSS?Yes. You can style SVGs with CSS or utility classes for full control.
Does it work with Markdown and HTML?Yes. SVGs integrate seamlessly with Markdown and HTML in your project.
